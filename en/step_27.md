## Arrange your shop

Lay everything out so the player knows what's what.

## Step 1

Drag your sprites into place on the stage.

The pizza shop puts the pizza in the middle, the chef and granny on the right, and the equipment along the bottom.

## Step 2

Drag each variable readout where you want it.

The pizza shop stacks `pizzas`{:class="block3variables"}, `pizzas per second`{:class="block3variables"} and `pizzas per click`{:class="block3variables"} top-left, puts `chef price`{:class="block3variables"} and `chefs`{:class="block3variables"} by the chef, and `granny price`{:class="block3variables"} and `grannies`{:class="block3variables"} by the granny.

## Tip

The score, prices, and other information shown on top of a game are often called the **HUD**, short for **heads-up display**.
